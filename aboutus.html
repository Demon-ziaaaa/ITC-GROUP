About US

Welcome to ITC Group which Formerly Known as INDIAN TRAILOR CORPORATION (Mumbai) Established in 1990, ITC Group has been a leading provider of bulk and heavy cargo transportation service in Mumbai and beyond with a strong foothold in the industry, we are known for our quality work and heavy duty transport Projects

Our strength lies in our Experienced Work Force backed by precise engineering, state-of-the-art equipment, and a strong commitment with safety being our prime concern.


WE MAKE IT FASTER

Wherever we are, we want to get somewhere else, and when we’ve got there, we want to be able to get there faster.


SAFE AND SECURE

Both safety and security are part of our ITC policies. We are proud to establish the safest and most secure portal for everyone’s relocation and shifting needs.


ON TIME DELIVERY

Deadline is a matter of utmost importance for us. We maintain a committed focus on the timely delivery of every project and have an outstanding track record.
